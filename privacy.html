Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

RoundFlow is built to respect your privacy. In short: we don't collect your data.

What we collect

Nothing. RoundFlow has no accounts, no servers and no analytics or tracking. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Your data stays on your device

Your timer profiles, settings and training history are stored only on your iPhone (and synced to your Apple Watch). This information never leaves your device and is never sent to us or anyone else.

Music & audio

RoundFlow lowers ("ducks") other apps' audio when it plays a bell or voice cue, using Apple's standard audio system. It does not read, access, or store your music, media library, or any data from other apps.

Apple Health

If you turn on "Save to Apple Health", RoundFlow writes your finished workouts (type, duration and an estimated active energy) to Apple Health on your device. This is optional and off by default. RoundFlow does not read any data from Apple Health, and your health data is never transmitted to us or any third party — it stays on your device under your control. You can revoke access anytime in the Health app or iOS Settings.

Purchases

RoundFlow Pro is purchased through Apple's App Store. All payments and subscription management are handled by Apple — we never see or store your payment details.

Children

RoundFlow does not collect data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, we'll update this page with a new date.
